## FAQ (for future landing page expansion)

**What is Credex?**
Credex is an AI billing platform that lets you buy AI tool credits in bulk at a discount. We pass 20% of that savings directly to you.

**How accurate are the savings estimates?**
We pull pricing directly from official vendor pages and update monthly. The savings numbers reflect real plan costs for your exact team size and tools.

**Do I need to switch tools?**
No. We recommend the most cost-effective plan for tools you're already using. You keep the same tools, just pay less.

**Is my data safe?**
Audit results are stored by a UUID — no email, no account required to see results. Your audit URL is only as private as the link you share.

**What tools do you support?**
ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Windsurf, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, and Gemini. More coming soon.
