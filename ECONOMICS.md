# Credex — Unit Economics

## Business Model

Credex earns revenue by acting as an AI-tool billing aggregator. When a company routes their AI tool purchases through Credex credits, Credex buys in bulk from vendors at a discount and passes 20% of that savings back to the customer, retaining the remainder as gross margin.

The free AI Spend Auditor is the top-of-funnel acquisition mechanism. Every audit creates a lead; every lead is a potential Credex credit customer.

---

## Pricing Model

| Customer pays via Credex | Credex buys from vendor | Gross margin |
|--------------------------|-------------------------|--------------|
| $1,000/mo                | ~$750/mo                | ~$250/mo (25%) |

Customer-facing discount: **20% off retail price**
Credex gross margin: **~25% of retail price** (bulk negotiation delta)

---

## Unit Economics (Per Customer)

Assumptions:
- Average AI tool spend per company: **$400/mo** (10-seat startup, 2–3 tools)
- Credex discount offered: **20%**
- Customer saves: **$80/mo → $960/yr**
- Credex retains: ~**$100/mo** gross from that customer

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Average customer monthly AI spend | $400 |
| Monthly savings to customer | $80 |
| Credex gross per customer/mo | ~$100 |
| CAC (audit funnel, SEO/content) | ~$0–$30 |
| LTV (12-month horizon, ~70% retention) | ~$840 |
| LTV:CAC ratio | >28× |

---

## Path to $1M ARR

$1M ARR = $83,333/mo gross revenue

At $100/mo per customer → **834 active paying customers**

Funnel math (conservative):
- 10,000 audits/mo at 2% conversion to Credex sign-up → **200 new customers/mo**
- Churn: 5%/mo → steady-state ~**4,000 customers** at scale
- At $100/mo gross each → **$400K MRR = $4.8M ARR**

$1M ARR is achievable at ~835 customers, requiring approximately:
- 42,000 audits (one-time) or
- 5,000 audits/mo sustained for 2 months at 2% conversion

---

## Key Drivers

1. **Audit volume** — every audit is a free lead. SEO, LinkedIn, HN, word-of-mouth.
2. **Conversion rate** — the audit result page is the sales page. High savings shown = high urgency.
3. **Retention** — once a company uses Credex credits for billing, switching cost is high (admin overhead, accounting integration).
4. **Expansion** — customers add more tools over time as their AI usage grows. ARPU expands naturally.

---

## Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Vendor pricing changes | Re-scrape monthly; audit engine uses latest data |
| Vendor bulk discount removed | Diversify across vendors; build direct relationships |
| Low conversion audit → credits | A/B test CTA copy; add case studies to results page |
| High CAC from paid acquisition | Prioritize organic (SEO, HN, LinkedIn) first |
