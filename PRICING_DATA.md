## Notes

- "Individual" plans are per-person subscriptions, not seat-licensed. Total company cost = plan price × number of users paying for it.
- API plans are usage-based; the audit engine uses user-supplied monthly spend for these tools.
- Enterprise pricing is estimated from public pricing pages or listed as custom where vendors require a sales conversation.
- All prices are in USD and represent monthly billing rates. Annual plans typically offer 15–20% discount; monthly prices used in the engine for conservative estimates.
